OUR COLLABORATIVE
PROCESS WITH CLIENTS

1. INITIAL CONSULTATION & PRE-PRODUCTION

  • We need first to establish a detailed overview of the product and the client's goal.
  • Our clients will have to share certain things that include product images, a CAD model, or any possible 3D model, dimensions of the product, and any preconceived idea or reference for rendering.

2. 3D ASSET CREATION & SETUP

  • After the inception of the production, we will have to focus on the 3D assets required during the process.
  • You can stay assured that the final assets looking and functioning the way you envisioned.

3. LIGHTING & STYLE DEVELOPMENT

  • The first focus is thrown on the camera angle and the animation sequence. The clients need to certify first that everything is going as per their planning.
  • Once approved, we take out time for the next important thing- lighting and developing the production's overall style.
  • At this point, you will be provided with preview renderings, which will allow you to experience and examine the texture and materials fixed for your product, thanks to photo-realistic studio-quality setups.

4. RENDERING & POST PRODUCTION

  • Executing the shot and applying the finishing touches sum up the final stage.
  • We ensure to give the illustration a subtle finishing touch, adding motion graphics, motion blur, lens flares, depth of need, all according to the requirements needed.
  • Our assistance doesn't cease here, for we will aid in making adjustments even after the final rendering.
